## Best “world standard” fields
For your dashboard, these benchmark types will work well:
- Football: FIFA ranking, opponent strength, competition stage.
- Athletics: personal best, season best, qualifying standard, score points.
- Rugby and basketball: match margin, tournament stage, ranking.
- Olympic sports: heat/round reached, qualification status, best result. [worldathletics](https://worldathletics.org/athletes/solomon-islands/sharon-firisua-14624966)
