## Data rules to enforce
- Keep one row per match, race, or event.
- Normalize dates to YYYY-MM-DD.
- Do not invent results.
- Preserve source URLs for every row.
- Use world_standard_type values that differ by sport, such as ranking, score points, personal best, or qualification stage. [worldathletics](https://worldathletics.org/athletes/solomon-islands/sharon-firisua-14624966)
